About Me

I am a B.Tech graduate in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, driven by a strong interest in building data-driven and intelligent systems. As a fresher, I am highly motivated to apply my academic knowledge and hands-on practice to solve real-world problems using data and machine learning techniques.

I have a solid foundation in Python and experience working with core data science libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn. I enjoy exploring datasets, performing data preprocessing and analysis, and developing machine learning models to uncover patterns and generate actionable insights. I am particularly interested in supervised and unsupervised learning techniques, model evaluation, and feature engineering.

My goal is to grow as an AI Engineer or Data Scientist by working on meaningful projects that combine data, algorithms, and real-world impact. I am continuously learning new concepts in machine learning, statistics, and AI, and I actively practice by building projects, experimenting with different models, and improving my problem-solving skills.

When I’m not working on code, I enjoy exploring emerging AI tools and trends, reading research articles and blogs, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing knowledge with the developer community. I believe in continuous learning, curiosity, and writing clean, well-structured code as I build my career in AI and data science.
